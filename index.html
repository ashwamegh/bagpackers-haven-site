It's time to experience Ziro! #ZFM2023

Ziro is a picturesque town in Arunachal Pradesh, India, known for its lush landscapes and as the cultural heart of the Apatani tribe. The Apatani people are renowned for their unique customs, including nose plugs and distinctive agricultural practices.

The Ziro Music Festival is an annual event held in Ziro, attracting music enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. It features live music performances by national and international artists against the backdrop of the stunning Eastern Himalayas. The festival celebrates the fusion of music, culture, and the natural beauty of the region, making it a must-visit event for travelers.
